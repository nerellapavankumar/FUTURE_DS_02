Customer Churn Analytics Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as Task 2 of the Future Interns Internship Program. The objective was to analyze customer subscription data, identify churn patterns, understand retention drivers, and provide actionable business insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze customer churn and retention trends.
Identify customer segments with high churn risk.
Understand factors influencing customer retention.
Evaluate revenue impact due to customer churn.
Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

📂 Dataset
Dataset: Telco Customer Churn Dataset
Total Customers: 7,043
Data Type: Customer subscription and service usage data

🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Power BI
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
Microsoft Excel
Data Visualization

📊 Dashboard Features
KPI Cards
Overall Churn Rate
Monthly Revenue at Risk
Average Charge (Churned Customers)
Retained Customers Percentage
Churn Analysis
Churn by Contract Type

Churn by Internet Service
Churn by Payment Method
Retention Analysis
Feature Impact on Churn Rate
Online Security Impact
Tech Support Impact
Online Backup Impact
Senior Citizen Analysis

🔍 Key Insights
Month-to-month customers have the highest churn rate.
Customers within the first 6 months are most likely to churn.
Electronic check users show higher churn compared to other payment methods.
Online Security and Tech Support significantly improve customer retention.
Long-term contracts reduce churn and increase customer loyalty.
Customer churn contributes significantly to revenue loss.

📈 Business Recommendations
Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts.
Improve onboarding experience for new customers.
Promote Online Security and Tech Support services.
Offer incentives for automatic payment methods.
Focus retention efforts on high-risk customer segments.
